How It Works

A simple 5-step payment flow your customers will love.

1

Customer Checks Out

Selects "Pay via UPI" at checkout and places the order.

2

QR Code Displayed

Thank-you page shows a QR code, UPI ID, and instructions.

3

Customer Pays

Scans QR in any UPI app and completes the transfer.

4

Confirms Payment

Enters UTR number, optionally uploads screenshot, clicks "I Have Paid".

5

Admin Approves

Verifies UTR in UPI app, clicks Approve. Order moves to Processing.
